Making Innovations, one petition at a time...
Here at REPChat, we aim to seamlessly connect you with a legislator of your choice. No matter who the legislator, whether your local chairman (Ryan McElveen) or your city mayor, REPChat will connect you with ease, no matter where you are.

Established in 2019

REPChat started as a simple idea from a hackathon at TJHSST in Alexandria, Virginia. Little did we know that this "idea" would become a reality, in the form of a political forum.

Our Goal

We aim to make political change and communication as easy as ever. At REPChat, dedicated citizens like you have the ability to petition requesting change without the bias of political ads on social media sites such as Facebook, Instagram, and WhatsApp.

Our Team

From Left to Right: Varun Vejalla, Amish Madhav, Krish Patel, Soham Ghandi.
We are a group of sophomores from Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology. We made REPChat at a hackathon, HackTJ 6.0. Surprisingly, we made our team a day before the competition, and started REPChat 20 hours before it was due. We are proud to say that we are leading political petitions, allowing citizens like you to communicate with politicians, even while they're busy.
